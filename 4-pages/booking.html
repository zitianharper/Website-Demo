About the Course

Join us for an immersive journey into the integration of EMDR with Art Therapy, a transformative approach designed to deepen therapeutic interventions and enhance client outcomes. ...

This small group event accommodates a maximum of ten participants to ensure personalised attention and interactive learning.

This will be most suited to EMDR therapists, practitioners and consultants looking to bring more art to their work, EMDR trained therapists working with children and Art therapists trained or training in EMDR looking to integrate the modalities.

Led by Lee Anna Simmons, an EMDR Europe accredited Consultant and HCPC registered Art Psychotherapist, this course synthesises theory, practice, and original material from her extensive experience. Lee designed and ran the first EMDR & Art Therapy courses (via EMDR UK and the British Association of Art Therapists) and supervision group (via the Institute of Group Analysis).

Participants will explore foundational concepts including relaxation exercises, resourcing techniques, bilateral stimulation with art, flash/blink technique, and even walking methods. Legal, ethical, and contextual discussions are included, along with hands-on experiences like history taking, setting boundaries, and resource building with art.

While this course won’t qualify you as an EMDR or Art Therapist, it helps you ethically use techniques from both modalities.

It also covers all eight phases of the EMDR protocol through an art therapy lens, with focus on interweaves, vicarious trauma, and self-care strategies.

CPD Credits: 10 hours Art Therapy / Psychotherapy CPD points, 9 EMDR Association UK and Europe CPD Points
